About Me

Rajiv Jagirdar was born and raised in the San Fernando Valley in Los Angeles County. After graduating from Granada Hills High School he attended The Stern School of Business at New York University and completed a B.S. in Finance and Marketing. He went on to work at BearingPoint as a Management Consultant and then fin-tech startup LendKey Technologies as the Manager of Financial Operations.


He is currently on a new career path and is taking a Coding Bootcamp through a UCLA Extention Program. He's looking forward to combining his previous experience in Finance and Operations with his newly expanding skillset in HTML, CSS and JavaScript for a bright career in technology.
